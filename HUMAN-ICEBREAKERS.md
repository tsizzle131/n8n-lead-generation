## Key Differences From Templates:

1. **Varied openings** - "Hey -", "Caught", "Noticed", "Saw" used naturally
2. **Different flow each time** - not following rigid formula
3. **Conversational transitions** - "Quick thing:", "Here's why:", "So here's the thing:"
4. **Natural phrasing** - sounds like real speech patterns
5. **Varied CTAs** - each asks differently
6. **Personal observations** - actually reference what makes each business unique
7. **Casual business opportunity framing** - not salesy

Each email feels like it's from someone who actually looked at the business and is reaching out personally, not from a template.
